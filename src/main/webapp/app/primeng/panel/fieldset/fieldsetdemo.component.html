PrimeNG is a collection of rich UI components for Angular. PrimeNG is a sibling of the popular JavaServer Faces Component Suite, PrimeFaces. All widgets are open source and free to use under MIT License. PrimeNG is developed by PrimeTek Informatics, a company with years of expertise in developing open source UI components.
